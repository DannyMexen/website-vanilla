    About

    Who we are and what we do are closely intertwined to create a culture of relevance and longevity. We believe in simplicity and elegance.

    Philosophy

    Arcarius Mexen means "Value Creation". We believe that people are at their best when they realize meaningful impact. Impact requires use of the right tools to get the job done in the best possible way.

    As such, we believe in building excellent, robust and secure applications that help businesses achieve their meaningful goals.

    Simplicity and elegance are at the core of everything we do because much like the products we build, our very approach is one grounded in engineering straightforwardness and beauty. Everything we do takes complexity and makes it simple, elegant.

    Experience

    AMWS was founded by Daniel M. Mwale, a software engineer passionate about providing simple, elegant solutions.

    Daniel has over 12 years of total experience writing software, managing projects and delivering products clients love to use.

    Community
